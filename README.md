## 📌 About
KishanSahayak is an Integrated Agriculture Support System designed to assist farmers in improving agricultural productivity and decision-making.

## 🚀 Features (Planned)
Goal & Objectives of Software

The main objectives of the system are:
•	To give fertilizer recommendations based on soil nutrients.
•	To increase awareness about safe pesticide usage and farmer health.
•	To provide information about government schemes and subsidies.
•	To promote digital empowerment among rural farmers.
•	Help farmers connect directly with buyers or markets to get better prices.
•	Reduce the dependency on middlemen and increase farmer income.
